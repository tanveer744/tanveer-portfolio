COURSE OVERVIEW
================================================================================

This specialized certification from DeepLearning.AI focuses on prompt engineering
techniques specifically for vision-based AI models. The course covers advanced
methods for effectively communicating with and controlling multimodal AI systems
that process both text and images.

As AI models increasingly incorporate vision capabilities, understanding how to
craft effective prompts for vision models has become essential for AI developers
and practitioners working with modern multimodal systems.
